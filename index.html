Tableau

Tableau is a powerful data visualization and business intelligence tool that enables users to analyze and present data in a visually compelling way. It is designed to help people see and understand their data by transforming it into interactive dashboards and reports.

Tableau has a user-friendly interface and provides extensive documentation and training resources, making it accessible to users with various levels of expertise. It is widely used by businesses, government organizations, and individuals to analyze data, make informed decisions, and communicate insights to stakeholders.
